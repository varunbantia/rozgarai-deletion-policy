Account and Data Deletion Policy – Rozgar AI

Effective Date: November 2025

Developer: Varun Bantia

How Users Can Delete Their Account

Rozgar AI allows users to permanently delete their account and all associated data directly within the app.

  • Open the Rozgar AI app.
  • Go to Menu (⋮)→ Settings → Delete Account.
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account and all related data will be permanently removed from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

What Data Gets Deleted

The following data is permanently deleted when you delete your account:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • App usage data (preferences, saved jobs, chat history)
  • Authentication data (login credentials)

Some limited information may be temporarily retained for fraud prevention or legal requirements and then automatically deleted.

Security
